Traditional Craftsmanship, Modern Living

At Timber & Grain, we honor time-honored woodworking techniques while designing furniture for contemporary lifestyles. Each piece is created by skilled artisans who understand the soul of wood.

We don't hide the natural character of wood—we celebrate it. Knots, grain variations, and subtle imperfections are what give our furniture its unique personality.

Our Materials

Natural Materials

We work exclusively with sustainable, natural materials that age beautifully.

Reclaimed Wood

Sustainably Harvested

Natural Finishes
