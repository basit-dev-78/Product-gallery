Our Heritage of Excellence

Founded in 1995, Elegance Bags began as a small atelier in Florence, Italy. Our founder, Giovanni Rossi, believed that true luxury lies in the details - the perfect stitch, the right leather, the thoughtful design.

Today, we continue this tradition, blending time-honored techniques with contemporary design to create bags that are both beautiful and functional. Each piece is a testament to our commitment to quality and craftsmanship.

We work with a select group of artisans who share our passion for excellence, ensuring that every bag that bears our name meets the highest standards of quality and design.
