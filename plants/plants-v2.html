Our Story

Founded in 2015, Evergreen Haven began as a small family-owned nursery with a passion for bringing the beauty of nature into urban homes. What started as a local plant shop has grown into an online destination for plant enthusiasts across the country.

Our mission is simple: to make plant ownership accessible, enjoyable, and rewarding for everyone. We believe that surrounding yourself with greenery improves well-being, reduces stress, and creates a more harmonious living environment.

Every plant in our collection is personally selected by our team of horticulturists for its health, beauty, and suitability for indoor living. We're not just selling plants - we're helping you create your own personal oasis.
