Our Story

Founded in 2010, Éclat began as a small boutique with a big vision: to bring timeless elegance and exceptional craftsmanship to modern fashion. Our founder, Isabelle Laurent, envisioned a space where women could find unique pieces that tell a story.

Today, we continue this tradition by working with talented designers and artisans from around the world. Each piece in our collection is carefully selected for its quality, design, and ability to make women feel confident and beautiful.

We believe in sustainable fashion and ethical production practices. Our commitment extends beyond style to making a positive impact on the world.
