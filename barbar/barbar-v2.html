FAQ

Need clarity before booking? Here are the answers clients ask most often.

Do I need to book in advance? +

We accept walk-ins when available, but we recommend booking to secure your preferred barber and time slot.

How long does an appointment take? +

Most cuts take 45 minutes. Combo services can take 60–75 minutes depending on detail and finish.

Do you offer beard-only services? +

Yes — Beard Architecture includes shaping, conditioning, and crisp line-ups with optional hot towel ritual.

Is membership cancel anytime? +

Yes. Memberships are billed monthly and can be canceled anytime. Perks apply through the active billing period.
