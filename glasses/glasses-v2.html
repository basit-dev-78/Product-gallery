Our Visionary Story

Founded in 2010, Visionary began with a simple mission: to create eyewear that combines exceptional craftsmanship with timeless design. Our founders, optical engineers and designers, were frustrated with the lack of quality options in the market.

Today, we're proud to be a leading name in premium eyewear, with over 50 stores worldwide and a loyal customer base that values both style and substance. Each pair of glasses is meticulously crafted in our workshops, where tradition meets innovation.

We believe that everyone deserves to see the world with clarity and style. That's why we work tirelessly to create frames that not only correct vision but also express individuality.
