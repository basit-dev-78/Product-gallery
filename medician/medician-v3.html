Important Safety Guidelines

Follow these essential guidelines to ensure safe and effective use of medications.

  • Always read the medication guide provided with your prescription or over-the-counter medicine.
  • Never share prescription medications with others, even if they have similar symptoms.
  • Store medications properly - away from heat, moisture, and direct sunlight.
  • Dispose of expired or unused medications safely - don't flush them down the toilet.
  • Always inform your healthcare provider about all medications and supplements you're taking.
